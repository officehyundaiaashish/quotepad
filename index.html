About This App

GroceryPro is a smart, offline-first grocery planner and quotation maker — built especially for daily household shopping and small kirana store estimates.

Whether you're heading to the sabzi mandi or preparing a monthly ration list, GroceryPro lets you build itemized lists with rates, quantities, and auto-calculated totals — then export a clean PDF quotation in one tap.

Key Features

Smart List Maker
Add items from your master inventory with qty + rate
Quotation PDF Export
One-tap export to clean A4 portrait PDF
Master Inventory
Manage all items, units & default rates in one place
Custom Units
Add Katta, Dozen, Box — any unit you need
100% Offline
No internet required. All data stored on your device
Dark Mode
Easy on the eyes at night or in low light
Backup & Restore
Export your full data as JSON, restore anytime
Works as App
Install as PWA for full app-like experience
